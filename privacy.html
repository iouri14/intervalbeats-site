Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

IntervalBeats ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

Summary

IntervalBeats does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information. There are no user accounts, no advertising, and no analytics tracking of individual users. The app runs locally on your device.

Information the App Accesses

Apple Music (optional)

If you choose to use Apple Music integration, IntervalBeats requests permission to access your Apple Music library and playback through Apple's MusicKit framework. This permission is used solely to play the playlists you select during a workout session. No music library data is transmitted to us or to any third party. You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Media & Apple Music.

Photo Library (optional)

If you choose to set a profile picture, IntervalBeats requests permission to access your photo library. The selected image is stored locally on your device only. It is not uploaded or shared.

Crash Reports

IntervalBeats uses Sentry to collect anonymous crash and error reports so we can diagnose and fix bugs. These reports may include:

  • Device model and iOS version
  • App version
  • Stack traces and error messages
  • A randomly generated, non-personal device identifier

Crash reports do not include your name, email, location, contacts, playlists, or workout history. Data is retained by Sentry for up to 30 days. You can learn more at sentry.io/privacy/.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name, email, phone number, address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Browsing history, advertising identifiers, or activity across apps
  • User profiles or accounts
  • Any data shared with advertisers

Data Storage

All workout presets, settings, and profile information are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, this data is removed.

Children's Privacy

IntervalBeats is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
